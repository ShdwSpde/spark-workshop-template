How to use it

Email is fastest

One question per email, descriptive subject line. If it's urgent, say so in the subject — we triage on that.

Your concierge channel · ready at Day 0

You'll get the private address in your welcome email. Placeholder:

concierge@spark.radicalimagination.xyz

Good questions for this channel

  • "My Grant Writer is pulling numbers from the wrong file — how do I constrain it?"
  • "I want to add a Volunteer Coordinator but my roster lives in Airtable — how?"
  • "My session protocol feels like busywork by Week 2 — what do I cut?"
  • "We're about to ship a grant that mentions a community we haven't consulted — what's the Validator call here?"

Questions that belong elsewhere

  • "Can you ghostwrite my newsletter?" — no, but the Newsletter Drafter agent does.
  • "Can you call my funder for me?" — no, relationships stay human.
  • "Can we extend concierge to 90 days?" — yes, for $500 extension or free via alumni tier (see Certificate & Alumni).
